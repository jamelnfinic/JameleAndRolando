About Dr. Norman Borlaug

Dr. Norman Borlaug was an American agronomist who is considered the "father of the Green Revolution". He received the Nobel Peace Prize in 1970 for his contributions to world peace through increasing food supply.

Dr. Borlaug's research and innovations in agriculture led to the development of high-yielding crop varieties and advanced agricultural practices, which helped to alleviate famine and hunger in many parts of the world.
